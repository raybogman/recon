Events

Opening  

Saturday 13/01/2022 : 10 pm - 7 am


For our launch we thought of something special.

We are holding an event to introduce the brand to you as a customer, we decided to have a flagship advertising event with the Company ADVANCED, who are a user centred based app that allows for a personalised and customisable advertising system.


For the opening we are using their digital infrastructure, that creates an Augmented Reality. Using our product as the first product of their app, it will allow for our customers to use the digital ad allows them to have VR and see themselves on the clothing of our Brand.


Both of our businesses pitch will take place are the grand store opening, where the store will be following their complete blanco style, to be transformed into the Digital AR store show the products on display, via customers phones.

Seminar - Basic Knowledge on Materials

Friday the 01/02/2022 : 7 am - 9 am 

Which materials should I preferably buy looking at the sustainable aspect ? How do I take care of certain materials? How can I make it last long? This and more questions will be answered in our talk about materials. It is essential to know how we can avoid toxic materials for planet and humans - and how to make them live long. Come and join us on Saturday night in our cozy atelier with delicous snacks and drinks and other lovely people that care about fashion.

(Inlcuded are a buffet with snacks and drinks)

adults : 25 Euros
students & under 18: 15 Euros

Up-cycling workshop

Sunday the 08/02/2022 : 11 pm - 16 am


create your own up-cycling item with us in our atelier!


This sunday event will be a special one. It is the first time that we offer a workshop for our beloved costumers. We want to invite you to experiment and gain insights into our production and how up-cycling pieces get created. We take you along through the process. From choosing materials to drawing the first draft into actually making it. By the end of this day you will have your first self made garment with memories of a lovely creative day!


Basic Sewing skills are required / 6 slots available

Per Person: 65 Euros (Including materials, lunch and drinks :))
